# Daily Checklist Explanation
## Pace

* Fast | 8mi (0.5mi/hr) | -5 to Navigate / PP, no foraging
* Normal | 6mi (0.38mi/hr)
* Slow | 4mi (0.25mi/hr) | +5 to Navigate / forage, can stealth

## Marching Order
* Single / two abreast
* d6: 1-2 single, 3-4 two abreast, 5-6 open

## Illumination
* Darkness - need light source
* Occasionally fungi /moss or other sources illuminate
* d6: 1-3 fungi / moss / faerzress, 4-6 darkness

## Navigation 
* Pace can affects navigation roll. -5 Fast | +5 Slow
* Survival DC 10 check
* If check fails you wander for 1d6 hours at a random direction before a check can be made again
* I make the roll taking into account the navigators skill check

## Mapping
* Mapper does not make rolls to forage and does not perceive threats
* Map can be used to not get lost if backtracking is needed
* Easiest way to map is with bubbles connecting different places/events encounters. Lines should annotate time span from one location to another (leave it to the players)

## Foraging
* Food - Each medium and small creature needs 1 pound food/day.
    * Half food ration: counts as half-day without food
    * No food: 3 + CON days without food, then +1 exhaustion/day
* Water - Each medium and small creature needs 1 gallon water/day.
    * Half Water: DC 15 CON or +1 exhaustion
    * No Water: +1 exhaustion
