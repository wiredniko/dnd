### Day 2: Ruins with Giant Fire Beetles
Marching Order: large open area
Illumination: dimly lit (glitter rocks)

* Ruins: Svirfneblin
	* *It's getting late, or more appropriately you are starting to get hungry, you start looking for a good place to rest, out of the oppressing darkness you see something glittering in the distance faintly* (pause...) *as you approach a little bit closer you see a large opening leading to a large cave, a defensive wall, now in ruins lays broken by the entrance of the cave, on the top of the cavern you see glittering sparkles illuminating a small town now in ruins*
    * Gnome and Terran writing
    * Town name: Glitter Rock (destroyed a long time ago, maybe 50 years or so)
    * Population: 50-75
    * POI: a broken fountain, now dry | a larger structure in the center of the town, has two stories, good vantage point | small graveyard, stacked rocks making a pyramid | no other bodies can be found (slaves)
    * 1d4 (3) trinkets if they explore
    * A bit of folded cloth that, when unfolded, turns into a stylish cap
    * A tiny sketch portrait of a goblin
    * A rectangular metal device with two tiny metal cups on one end (Investigation DC: 15 throws sparks when wet)
* **Giant Fire Beetles**: 3d6 (11) - MM 325 - CR 0
    * Can harvest glowing glands of beetles as a light source. (Survival DC: 10 to extract)
